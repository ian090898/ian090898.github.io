The div_relative class element has position: absolute, and will remain in the natural flow of the page

The div_absolute class element has position: absolute, and will NOT remain in the natural flow of the page.

The div_fixed_2 element has position: fixed, and will NOT remain in the natural flow of the page. It will position itself according to the viewport. It will also act as anchor point for the absolutely positioned div_absolute element:

position: fixed, bottom: 0, left: 0, right: 0.

A sticky element toggles between relative and fixed, depending on the scroll position. It is positioned relative until a given offset position is met in the viewport - then it "sticks" in place (like position:fixed).
